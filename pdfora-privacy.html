Pdfora Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Pdfora respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information when you use Pdfora.

1. About Pdfora

Pdfora is a mobile app that helps users:

  • Convert images to PDF
  • Export PDF pages as images
  • Compress PDF files
  • Sign PDF documents

2. Information We Collect

Pdfora is designed to process selected files locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account.

Files you select, create, save, or share using the app are handled on-device as part of app functionality.

3. Files and Storage Access

Pdfora uses Android system file picker and save dialogs so you can choose files and save output files. The app only accesses files you explicitly select.

4. Ads and Third-Party Services

Pdfora may display ads using Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may collect certain data, such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device and app information
  • Diagnostics information
  • Approximate location derived from IP address

This information may be used for ad delivery, analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement.

For more information, please review Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

Pdfora does not sell your personal information. Data handled by third-party advertising services such as AdMob is subject to their own policies.

6. Data Retention

Files created through Pdfora remain on your device or in the location you choose unless you delete them. Pdfora does not maintain user accounts or store your files on its own servers.

7. Children’s Privacy

Pdfora is not specifically directed to children.

8. Security

We use platform-provided file access mechanisms to reduce unnecessary access to device storage. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support.syntoralabs@gmail.com
