Privacy Policy for Dhinam Oru Kural

This Privacy Policy explains how Dhinam Oru Kural handles user and device information.

Dhinam Oru Kural is designed as a simple offline-first application that provides daily Thirukkural content and related meanings.

The app does not collect personal information directly from users.

However, the app or website may use third-party services such as Google AdSense or Google AdMob, which may collect certain information used to identify your device or improve advertising services.

Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Third-party advertising or analytics services may collect information including, but not limited to:

  • Device information
  • Browser or app usage data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location information
  • Cookies or similar technologies

How This Information Is Used

This information may be used to:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze app and website performance
  • Improve user experience

Offline Content

Most Thirukkural content used in the app is stored locally within the application to provide a fast and offline reading experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or directly share personal information with third parties except where necessary for integrated services such as advertising or analytics platforms.

Your Choices

Children’s Privacy
